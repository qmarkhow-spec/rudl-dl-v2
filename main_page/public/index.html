Core Features

We integrate all the tools you need for app distribution into one concise platform.

Secure App Storage

Easily upload your IPA / APK files. Each app has its own workspace, maintaining a complete version history and metadata to eliminate version control chaos.

Flexible Download Management

Generate easy-to-share short URLs for your apps, customize multi-language download pages, and precisely control the activation status of each version to ensure users always get the right one.

Real-time Usage Monitoring

The dashboard provides key data such as daily downloads and point consumption, with configurable alerts for anomalies, keeping you informed of your distribution status and cost-effectiveness.

Seamless Team Collaboration

Invite your team members, developers, or partners to share resources and centralize all notifications and instructions, improving communication efficiency.

Quick Start

Three steps to start your app distribution:

  1. Create Project: After registering, create your first application project.
  2. Upload App: Drag and drop your IPA or APK file into the console.
  3. Share Link: Generate a download link and share it with your testers or team.
